# アプリアイコン生成プロンプト

デザイン系AIに依頼するためのプロンプト集。

---

## 1. メインアイコン（iOS / Android 共通）

> Mobile app icon design for an iOS/Android app called "休肝日つくーる" (Alcohol-Free Day Maker) — a casual habit-tracking app that helps users take regular days off from drinking.
>
> **Concept:** A beer mug with a friendly diagonal slash line across it, meaning "no drinks today." The slash should NOT look like a prohibition sign (⊘) — instead, use a soft, rounded diagonal line to keep the tone positive and approachable.
>
> **Visual details:**
> - A simple, recognizable beer mug (glass with handle) as the main element
> - Amber/golden beer visible inside the glass
> - A diagonal slash line (top-left to bottom-right) in green (#4CAF50 ~ #66BB6A), representing a positive "rest day" rather than a harsh "forbidden"
> - The slash should have rounded ends and be thick enough to read at small sizes
>
> **Color palette:**
> - Background: blue gradient (#5BA3E8 → #3A7BC8)
> - Glass: white/translucent
> - Beer: warm amber/gold
> - Slash: green (success/positive tone)
>
> **Style:** Flat design with minimal gradients. Clean, modern, friendly. No text. Should be clearly readable at 60×60px (home screen icon size). Avoid medical/clinical feel — this is a casual habit-tracking app, not a health warning.
>
> **Format:** 1024×1024px, PNG, with iOS rounded-rectangle safe zone in mind (keep key elements away from corners).

## 2. Android Adaptive Icon — 前景（foreground）

> Based on the main app icon above, create the **foreground layer only** for an Android adaptive icon.
>
> - Transparent background (no solid color, no gradient behind)
> - Beer mug + green slash only, centered on the canvas
> - The content must fit within the **center 66%** of the canvas (safe zone). Android crops adaptive icons into circles, rounded squares, etc. — anything outside the inner 66% may be clipped.
> - Same style, colors, and proportions as the main icon
>
> **Format:** 1024×1024px, PNG with transparent background.

---

## 3. Android Adaptive Icon — 背景（background）

> Create the **background layer only** for an Android adaptive icon.
>
> - Blue gradient fill (#5BA3E8 at top → #3A7BC8 at bottom)
> - Optional: a subtle radial glow or soft light in the center for depth (white, ~5% opacity)
> - No icons, no text — just the gradient
>
> **Format:** 1024×1024px, PNG.

---

## 4. Android モノクロアイコン（Material You テーマ用）

> Create a **monochrome silhouette** version of the app icon for Android Material You themed icons.
>
> - Single color: solid black on transparent background
> - Beer mug silhouette (filled) with handle + diagonal slash
> - Must be recognizable as the same app at a glance
> - Clean edges, no gradients, no shading
>
> **Format:** 1024×1024px, PNG with transparent background. Black (#000000) silhouette only.

---

## 5. スプラッシュスクリーン用アイコン

> Create a **standalone version** of the app icon for use on a splash/loading screen.
>
> - Transparent background (no rounded rectangle, no background color)
> - Beer mug in the app's primary blue (#4A90D9 ~ #3A7BC8) instead of white
> - Amber/gold beer inside
> - Green diagonal slash (#4CAF50 ~ #66BB6A)
> - Slightly more detailed than the app icon since it displays larger on screen
>
> **Format:** 1024×1024px, PNG with transparent background.

## デザインの背景

- 「休肝日」＝お酒を飲まない日。肝臓を休める日。
- ターゲットは「禁酒」ではなく「適度に休む」ユーザー。ストイックではなくカジュアル。
- 緑のスラッシュは禁止ではなく「今日はお休み」というポジティブな意味合い。
- 医療・健康警告の雰囲気は避ける。親しみやすさ重視。
